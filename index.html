Intro

Being one of the best high volume online poker professionals in our planet’s history allowed me the time and opportunity to explore and learn all that the digital frontier had to offer. After 10+ years as a successful internet entrepreneur, trader, and data miner, I experienced something amazing: building with great teams at peer to peer learning systems.

While I do find lower layer memory manipulation, hardware and data architecture fascinating, most of my recent projects have been more practical web apps done in 1-2 weeks at App Academy displaying my coding proficiency in JavaScript and Python via frameworks such as React, Redux, Flask, and Express. I've also recently spearheaded a production MVP build with a few friends from a/A, attempting to integrate graphQL and smart contracts into OPBets.

I've always preached understanding of computerized gaming solutions before all else and quickly learned to apply that knowledge to filling inefficiencies in other markets. Through that journey, I've seen first hand the transition of artificial intelligence from traditional tree type AI to ML regressed upon solutions. That era remains a big part of why I wanted to move towards being a full time software engineer.
